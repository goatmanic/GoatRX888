RX888 mkII gain controls exposed to Soapy/Gqrx:

ATT  - PE4304 attenuator in the HF branch; active in HF mode.
LNA  - R828D LNA; active in VHF mode.
MIX  - R828D mixer gain; active in VHF mode.
IF   - R828D IF VGA; active in VHF mode.
VGA  - external AD8370; active in both HF and VHF modes.

The Gqrx hardware-AGC button controls the R828D LNA and mixer only.
Gqrx may visually disable every slider while hardware AGC is enabled.
